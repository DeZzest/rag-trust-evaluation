DEMO NATIONAL UNIVERSITY
ADMISSION RULES 2023

Section 1. General Provisions

1.1 Admission campaign begins on July 10, 2023.

Section 2. Required Documents

2.1 Passport copy.
2.2 Secondary education certificate.

Section 3. Admission Criteria

3.1 Admission score is calculated as:
	- 60% National Entrance Exam
	- 40% GPA

3.2 Minimum admission score: 70/100.

Section 4. Tuition Fees

4.1 Annual tuition fee for Bachelor's programs: 30,000 UAH.
