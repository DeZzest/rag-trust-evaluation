EXPULSION POLICY

Section 1. Grounds for Expulsion

1.1 Academic debt in more than 5 subjects.
1.2 Three academic integrity violations.
1.3 Failure to pay tuition fee within 60 days.

Section 2. Procedure

2.1 The decision is made by the Academic Council.
2.2 The student has the right to appeal within 10 days.
