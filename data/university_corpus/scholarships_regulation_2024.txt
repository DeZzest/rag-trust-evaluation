SCHOLARSHIP REGULATION 2024

Section 1. Academic Scholarship

1.1 Students with GPA ≥ 90 receive 2000 UAH per month.
1.2 Students with GPA ≥ 95 receive 3000 UAH per month.

Section 2. Social Scholarship

2.1 Students from vulnerable categories receive 2500 UAH per month.

Section 3. Scholarship Termination

3.1 Scholarship is terminated if GPA falls below 85.
3.2 See Academic Integrity Policy Section 2 for violations.
