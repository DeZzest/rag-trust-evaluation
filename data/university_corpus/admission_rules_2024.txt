DEMO NATIONAL UNIVERSITY
ADMISSION RULES 2024

Section 1. General Provisions

1.1 These Admission Rules regulate the admission process for Bachelor's and Master's programs in 2024.

1.2 The admission campaign begins on July 1, 2024.

1.3 Applicants must submit required documents via the official admission portal.

Section 2. Required Documents

2.1 Passport copy.
2.2 Secondary education certificate.
2.3 Motivation letter (minimum 500 words).
2.4 English proficiency certificate (B1 or higher).

Section 3. Admission Criteria

3.1 Admission score is calculated as:
	- 50% National Entrance Exam
	- 30% GPA
	- 20% Motivation Letter evaluation

3.2 Minimum admission score: 75/100.

Section 4. Tuition Fees

4.1 Annual tuition fee for Bachelor's programs: 35,000 UAH.
4.2 Annual tuition fee for Master's programs: 45,000 UAH.
